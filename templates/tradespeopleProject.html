Tradespeople Near Me

Project Details

Description:

Tradespeople Near Me was my final year project in university and took a substantial amount of time to research, design and develop. It is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) which enables people to locate various professionals in their area for a particular problem. It provides tradespeople with an opportunity to advertise their services in the form of a tailored web page, containing helpful information such as an about section, pictures of previous work and contact information. It is also be beneficial to the consumer as it will display the diameter in which each individual tradesperson is willing to travel to carry out a job, the hours they are willing to work and the estimated cost of their services. Not only this but it provides consumers with the ability to favourite tradespeople for easy and offline access. Therefore, it allows you to call your favourited tradespeople regardless of your internet connectivity.

Development

Evaluation

Tradespeople Near Me was arguably my toughest project to date as it involved using complex technologies and algorithms to produce the end product. From a technical point of view, the project uses Django, Docker, Nginx, Digital Ocean and Postgres and so was tough to configure in a relatively short space of time. On top of this, the Haversine formula algorithm was implemented to calculate the distance between the consumer and each available tradesperson. Each of these technologies are covered in depth in my attached thesis. Considering the learning curve associated with each of this technologies, I am very pleased with its outcome. From a user interface perspective, I am happy with the project's overall look and operation, however, I am would've liked more development time to improve it, with this being a scarcity during a very busy final year.

Tradespeople Near Me was broken down into stages so that the best possible end product could be achieved. This is clearly seen when comparing the application to its first prototype. I am proud that each originally designed functionality was incorporated into the app. however, given more time, I would've liked to have added a smart blockchain based contract between consumers and tradespeople to verify business between the two parties.
